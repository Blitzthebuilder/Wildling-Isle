HOW TO PLAY

Arrows / WASD move  ·  Z or Enter = A (confirm / talk, hold to fast-forward)  ·  X = B (back)  ·  C = team, twice = Wildling-dex.
Walk in tall grass to meet wild Wildlings. Toss Charm Orbs to befriend them. Rest at the cabin or campfires to heal.
Progress auto-saves where the browser allows. For a bullet-proof backup, tap CODE in-game to copy a save code, and tap it on the title screen to load one.
Wildlings evolve twice as they level up. Defeat your rival Briar at each gate to reach new areas — beat him at the end of the pier to become Champion!
