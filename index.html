HOW TO PLAY

Arrows / WASD move  ·  Z or Enter = A (confirm / talk, hold to fast-forward)  ·  X = B (back)  ·  C cycles team → Wildling-dex → Ranch.
Your team holds 6 Wildlings — extra catches go to Doc Bramble's Ranch, where you can deposit and withdraw team members anytime.
Battles pay coins — spend them at the cabin or campfires on orbs, Sweet Berries, and Bramble Jam (full heal + cure). Watch out for Burn, Shock, and being put On Ice!
Save up for the Bramble Bike (any shop, 150c) — then press B out in the world to hop on and cover ground twice as fast.
Boothbay Harbor has specialists: the Move Tutor teaches four powerful songs for every type — pick the Wildling and see its options — and old Eldon in the west house is said to keep something ancient for a true champion.
A full moveset means choices: when a 5th move comes along, you decide what to forget — and the ancient obelisk in the meadow lets Wildlings remember moves they gave up.
Walk in tall grass to meet wild Wildlings. Toss Charm Orbs to befriend them. Rest at the cabin or campfires to heal.
Progress auto-saves where the browser allows. For a bullet-proof backup, tap CODE in-game to copy a save code, and tap it on the title screen to load one.
Wildlings evolve twice as they level up. Defeat your rival Briar at each gate to reach new areas — beat him at the end of the pier to become Champion!
